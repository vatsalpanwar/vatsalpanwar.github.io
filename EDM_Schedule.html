Exoplanets and Disks Meeting


The Exoplanets and Disks Meeting (EDM) is a weekly meeting at the Anton Pannekoek Institute in Amsterdam, where topics related to exoplanets, disks and planet formation are discussed. The meeting takes place on Tuesdays from 11:00 until 12:30. An overview of previous (from 2016 onwards) and upcoming talks is shown below. If you are interested in attending a meeting and/or giving a talk, please send an e-mail to Gabriela Muro Arena or Vatsal Panwar.
